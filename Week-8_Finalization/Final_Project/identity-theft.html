Introduction

Identity theft occurs when someone steals your personal information, such as your name, Social Security number, or credit card details, to commit fraud or other crimes. It's a growing cyber threat that can lead to financial loss, ruined credit, and emotional distress.

How It Works

Identity theft typically starts with methods like phishing (trick emails or websites), malware (viruses), data breaches, dumpster diving, or shoulder surfing. Once data is obtained, criminals use it for fraud.

Where It Occurs & Real-Life Examples

Vulnerable places: online, offline, at work, in public. Examples include Equifax breach, phishing attacks, and lost wallets leading to fraud.
